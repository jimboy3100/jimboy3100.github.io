🛡️ Anti-Teaming System — Discrete Events

This server uses a discrete event-based anti-teaming system. Instead of a single bucket that accumulates forever, every action is tracked as a separate event with its own lifespan. When an event expires, its penalty is removed — older actions don't get extended by newer ones.

Penalty Sources

Each of these actions adds a discrete anti-team event to your score:

  • ☣ Virus (pop) — +32 penalty, lasts 32 seconds. Triggered when you eat a virus.
  • ◉➚◉ Split — +10 penalty, lasts 10 seconds. Triggered each time you split.
  • ⬤ W (eject mass) — +1 penalty per cell, lasts 1 second. Triggered each time you press W. If you have 16 cells, one W press = +16.
  • ⚠ Danger Zone — +0.1 per cell every 0.2 seconds, batched every 4 seconds, lasts 8 seconds. Only applies when the map is contracting and your cells are in the outer ring.
  • ∞ Base Decay — always active, never expires. This is the natural mass loss. It's a flat percentage of your mass — a 10,000 mass cell loses ~80, a 100 mass cell loses ~0.8. The base rate scales dynamically with player count using a square-root curve: −0.8%/4s with ≤32 players (vanilla Agar.io rate, verified from 97,806 real packets), decreasing smoothly as more players join: 64 players → 0.57%, 128 → 0.40%, 256 → 0.28%, 512 → 0.20%, 1024 → 0.14%. Formula: decay = 0.8% × √(32 / players) when players > 32.

How It Works

Your total anti-team score is the sum of all currently active events. When the total exceeds the threshold (35), your cells start decaying faster. The decay multiplier formula is:

decay = base × (1 + score / 15)

At exactly 35, no penalty is applied (the check is strictly greater than). At 50 you'd lose 3.5× normal decay. At 100 you'd lose 7.7× normal decay.

Key: Each event expires independently. If you eat a virus at t=0 and another at t=20, the first virus event expires at t=32 and the second at t=52. They don't extend each other. Once all events expire, your score returns to 0 and decay goes back to normal.

Live Stats HUD — Reading the Display

When connected to Legend FFA, you can see your live anti-team status in the stats bar. Here is an example of what it looks like:

⚗ Anti: (74.0/35.0, −4.7%/4s) | ☣ Virus: 2 (+64, ⏳32s) | ◉➚◉ Split: 1 (+10, ⏳7s) | ∞ −0.8%/4s

What each part means:

  • ⚗ Anti: (74.0/35.0, −4.7%/4s) — Your anti-team score is 74.0 (above the 35.0 threshold). The −4.7%/4s is your actual total mass loss — you're losing 4.7% of your mass every 4 seconds. When below threshold, the score appears in green and no extra decay rate is shown.
  • ☣ Virus: 2 (+64, ⏳32s) — You have 2 active virus events contributing +64 to your total score (32 each). ⏳32s = the last virus event expires in 32 seconds. Earlier virus events may expire sooner — this shows only the latest one.
  • ◉➚◉ Split: 1 (+10, ⏳7s) — 1 active split event contributing +10. Expires in 7 seconds.
  • ⬤ W: 3 (+48, ⏳1s) — 3 active W events. Expire very quickly (1 second each).
  • ⚠ Zone 120s (+10, ⏳8s) — You're in the danger zone (120s left in the phase). The danger zone has added +10 to your anti-team score. The oldest danger batch expires in 8 seconds.
  • ∞ −X%/4s — The dynamic base decay. Always present. The rate depends on how many players are alive: 0.8%/4s with ≤32 players (vanilla), decreasing with more players (e.g. 0.57% at 64, 0.40% at 128, 0.28% at 256). This is the natural game decay.

⏳ Important: "Last Expires" Explained

The ⏳ timer shows when the last (most recent) event of that type expires. It does NOT mean all events of that type expire at once.

Example: You eat a virus at t=0 and another at t=20. The display might show ☣ Virus: 2 (+64, ⏳32s). The ⏳32s is the second virus's timer. The first virus actually expires at t=32 (12 seconds from now), while the second expires at t=52 (32 seconds from now). When the first expires, the display updates to ☣ Virus: 1 (+32, ⏳12s).

📊 Enable / Disable the Display

The decay stats are enabled by default in Legend Mod. To toggle them:

  1. Open Settings (gear icon)
  2. Find the Stats section
  3. Toggle "Decay Info (LegendWorld)" on or off

This setting only affects Legend FFA. It has no effect on other servers.
