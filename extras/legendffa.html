🛡️ Anti-Teaming System

This server uses the real Agar.io anti-teaming algorithm — the same penalty-based decay system used by official servers. It tracks a per-player anti-team score that accumulates from teaming behaviors:

  • Splitting adds +10 penalty per split (teamers split constantly to feed each other)
  • Ejecting mass (W) adds +1 penalty per eject (mass trading is a core teaming tactic)
  • Eating teammates' cells adds penalty based on mass consumed
  • Popping (virus) adds +10 penalty when you get virus-popped (prevents intentional pop-feeding)

When your anti-team score exceeds 35, your cells start decaying faster — the higher your score, the faster you shrink. The score naturally decreases over time (−4 per tick) when you stop teaming, so solo players are never affected.

This means teamers will rapidly lose mass while solo players enjoy a completely fair game. No manual bans needed — the system is fully automatic.
